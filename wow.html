🏛️ Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz, is a white marble mausoleum that blends Persian, Islamic, and Indian styles.

"A teardrop on the cheek of time." – Rabindranath Tagore
📍 Agra, Uttar Pradesh, India
Fun Fact

Over 20,000 artisans worked on its construction between 1632 and 1653.

🧱 Great Wall of China

Built across centuries, the Great Wall stretches over 21,000 km and served as a fortress against invasions.

"He who has not climbed the Great Wall is not a true man." – Mao Zedong
📍 Northern China, from Dandong to Lop Lake
Trivia

Contrary to myth, the Wall is not visible from the Moon with the naked eye.

✝️ Christ the Redeemer

This iconic statue, standing 30 meters tall atop Mount Corcovado, symbolizes peace and hospitality.

⛰️ Machu Picchu

An ancient Incan citadel in Peru, surrounded by cloud forests and mystery.
