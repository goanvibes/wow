WOW One: Wonders of the World

An interactive journey into the greatest human-made and natural wonders... By Sergio, Khaliza, Samiya, Joldrich, and Mikhail.

Welcome to Our Digital Museum

Welcome to WOW One, our group's ambitious project to chronicle the most awe-inspiring sights on Earth. This website is a testament to what can be built with the foundational language of the web: pure HTML. We explore the line where history, architecture, and nature converge to create something truly magical.

Our theme covers the celebrated New7Wonders of the World, which are human-made marvels, and touches upon breathtaking natural wonders that leave us humbled.

This is a group project for our Web Development course.
